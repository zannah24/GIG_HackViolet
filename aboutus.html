About Us

GIT IT GIRL is a website dedicated to helping women in tech find job opportunities, connect with others in their field through events, keep themselves updated, upskill themselves, and ultimately become empowered.

Our mission is to create a supportive community where women can learn, grow, and thrive in their tech careers. We believe that by providing access to resources, events, and networking opportunities, we can help women overcome the challenges they face in the tech industry.

Whether you're just starting out in your tech career or looking to take the next step, GIT IT GIRL is here to support you every step of the way. Join our community today and start achieving your goals!
